Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How do I know my donation actually reaches the people in need? +

We publish detailed field reports, photos, and financial breakdowns for every campaign. 98% of all donated funds reach beneficiaries within 48–72 hours, verified by our field teams on the ground.

Can I donate on behalf of someone else or as a gift? +

Absolutely! During the donation process you can specify a recipient name. We'll issue a personalized donation certificate that makes a beautiful and meaningful gift.

How can I volunteer with Helping Hearts? +

Send us a message through this form or email us directly. We have volunteering opportunities in field support, campaign verification, social media, and fundraising events across Pakistan.

Can my organization partner with Helping Hearts? +

Yes! We actively partner with NGOs, hospitals, schools, and corporate CSR programs. Select "Partnership / Collaboration" in the contact form above or email us directly to discuss how we can work together.

Is my payment information secure? +

Yes. All payment processing on Helping Hearts is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. We do not store card details — payments are processed by verified, PCI-compliant payment gateways.
